```
   § 01.  ABOUT
  ─────────────────────────────────────────
```

I build interfaces with clarity, rhythm, and character.

I care about the decisions most people never notice but always feel —
the spacing, the weight of a typeface, the timing of a transition, the
moment a product stops feeling like software and starts feeling like
something someone made with intention.

My work sits somewhere between utility and personality. I'm drawn to
interfaces that have a point of view — things that feel designed rather
than assembled. I approach the frontend the way a craftsperson
approaches their material: slowly, deliberately, and always asking
whether it could be better.

25 public repositories. Still building. Still refining.
